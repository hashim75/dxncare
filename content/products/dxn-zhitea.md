The functional logic behind the base</h2><p>‍</p><h3 id=\"\">Fermentation is more than flavor.</h3><p>‍</p><p id=\"\">Fermentation alters the chemistry of tea, breaking down molecules and producing organic acids and peptides that aid digestion and enhance flavor.

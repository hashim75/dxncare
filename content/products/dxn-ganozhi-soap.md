This acid mantle serves as:</p><p>‍</p><ul id=\"\"><li id=\"\">a microbial control mechanism that discourages pathogenic overgrowth,</li><li id=\"\">an enabler of lipid-processing enzymes that construct and repair the barrier, and</li><li id=\"\">a key factor in preserving stratum corneum cohesion and desquamation.</li></ul><p>‍</p><p id=\"\">When you cleanse with strongly alkaline products (many traditional bar soaps), the skin’s pH is lifted toward neutrality or alkalinity. That shift temporarily disables key enzymes involved in barrier maintenance, increasing TEWL and leaving the skin tight, flaky, or prone to irritation and infection.</p><p>‍</p><p id=\"\">DXN Ganozhi Soap is formulated to avoid that outcome. The pH-balancing ingredients are there to keep the skin within its physiological range.

The definitive test is the <strong>3:00 AM Check</strong>.</p><ul id=\"\"><li id=\"\"><strong id=\"\">If 3:00 AM sugar is LOW (<70 mg/dL):</strong> It's the Somogyi Effect. You need <em>less</em> medication or a bedtime snack.</li><li id=\"\"><strong id=\"\">If 3:00 AM sugar is NORMAL/HIGH:</strong> It's the Dawn Phenomenon.
